### 1.4 Time & Work

**Concept:** If A can do work in 'x' days, A's 1 day work = 1/x

### 1.7 Ages

**Example:**
> Father's age is 3 times son's age. After 10 years, it will be 2 times. Find present ages.

**Solution:**

### 1.8 Probability

**Formula:** P(E) = Favorable outcomes / Total outcomes

**Example:**
> Probability of getting a sum of 7 with two dice?

**Solution:**

> Total outcomes = 36  
> P = 6/36 = 1/6
